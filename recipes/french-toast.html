French Toast

Description

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Butter

How to make

  1. Step 1Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Step 3Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Step 4Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Step 5Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Step 6Serve hot.
